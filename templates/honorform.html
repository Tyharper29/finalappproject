Welcome to the HONOR Study questionnaire

Thank you for your interest in the HONOR study. The survey below consists of questions about your eating behaviors. Completing this survey is voluntary, and you may stop at any time. We realize that disclosing sensitive or personal information can cause discomfort, and want you to know that we appreciate your cooperation and that you may stop at any time. The survey should take about five minutes. If you are still eligible after completing the survey, we will send you an email with instructions for the next step in the recruitment process. When you click on “Submit” at the bottom of the page, you give your permission for us to contact you about the survey.

Thank you for taking the time to complete this survey.
